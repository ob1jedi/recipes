Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
 - 1 Quarter cup milk
 - Brown bread
 - Italian Herbs (usually comes in a bottle)

Directions:
  1. Whisk the eggs, milk and oil together
  2. Sprinkle Italian Herbs and Spices into the mixture.
  3. Dip the bread in for only 2 seconds on each side and then fry the bread.
